Garlic Noodles Recipe

Who knew such a humble vegetable could pack so much flavour? If you're low on ingredients, this recipe for garlic noodles is a great way to satisfy your taste buds. Recipe from recipetineats.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Oyster sauce
  • Maggi seasoning
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Fish sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles. Reserve some of the noodle water for later.
  2. Drain the noodles.
  3. Saute the garlic with melted butter.
  4. Add fish sauce, Maggi seasoning, Oyster sauce, and the reserved noodle water from earlier. Stir thoroughly.
  5. Add the noodles and cheese.
  6. Serve with whatever else you have in your kitchen. Enjoy!
