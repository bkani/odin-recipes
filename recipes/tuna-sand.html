Tuna Sandwich Recipe

Description

Tuna and corn: a heavenly combination of cheap yet delicious ingredients. Put them between two slices of bread and you've got the sandwich of a lifetime. Recipe from What Dad Cooked.

Ingredients

  • Can of tuna
  • Can of corn
  • Chopped celery
  • Chopped chives
  • Chopped dill pickle
  • Crisps
  • Lemon
  • Lettuce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Sliced bread

Steps

  1. Mix the tuna, corn, celery, pickle, and mayonnaise with a fork.
  2. Add lots of lemon.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toast bread.
  5. Assemble the sandwich.
  6. Serve with chips.
